Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

Who We Are

Car Commercial Residential Emergency Locksmith Service, LLC ("CCR Locksmith," "we," "our," "us") provides automotive, residential, commercial, and emergency locksmith services in Metro Detroit.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide (name, phone, email).
  • Service details (location, lock issue, property/vehicle details needed for service).
  • Communication records when you call, email, or submit a contact request.
  • Basic website analytics data (for example, page visits and device/browser data) via analytics tools.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to requests, schedule and perform locksmith services.
  • To confirm authorization or ownership when required for security-sensitive services.
  • To send estimates, invoices, warranty-related updates, or service follow-up communication.
  • To improve website content, service quality, and customer support.

When We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only when needed to operate our business (for example, payment processing, website hosting/analytics, or legal compliance), and only for legitimate business purposes.

Data Retention

We retain records for as long as reasonably necessary for service delivery, warranty support, accounting/tax obligations, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Your Choices

  • You may request updates or corrections to contact information you provided.
  • You may request deletion of data where deletion is permitted by law and operational requirements.
  • You can stop receiving non-essential communications by contacting us directly.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No internet transmission or storage method is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

Our services and website are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at carcommercialresidentiallocks@gmail.com or call (313) 889-3266.
