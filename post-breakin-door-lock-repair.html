We Secure Your Property Immediately

A break-in is a traumatic event that leaves your property vulnerable. CCR Locksmith provides rapid response post-break-in repair services to secure your home or business. We don't just patch things up; we repair or replace damaged hardware to ensure your property is safe again.

Our technicians are equipped to handle forced entry damage, including kicked-in doors, broken jambs, and destroyed locks. We can also recommend and install high-security upgrades to prevent future incidents.

Our Repair Services Include:

  • Door Frame Repair: Reinforcing split or broken door jambs.
  • Lock Replacement: Installing new, higher-security locks.
  • Door Replacement: Sourcing and hanging new doors if repair isn't possible.
  • Security Upgrades: Installing strike plates, wrap-around plates, and deadbolts.
  • Board-Up Service: Temporary securing of windows or doors if parts are needed.

Prevent Future Break-ins

  • High-Security Locks: Pick and drill resistant cylinders.
  • Reinforced Hardware: Heavy-duty strike plates and long screws.
  • Security Bars: Additional protection for windows and glass doors.
  • Smart Security: Cameras and alarms to deter intruders.
