Smart Locks vs. Traditional Locks: Which is Right for You?

By CCR Locksmith Team • Published December 12, 2025 • Updated March 4, 2026

Smart Locks: Pros and Tradeoffs

  • Pros: keyless access, temporary codes, activity logs, remote control options.
  • Tradeoffs: battery dependence, app setup, and compatibility considerations.

Traditional Deadbolts: Pros and Tradeoffs

  • Pros: mechanical reliability, simplicity, lower maintenance.
  • Tradeoffs: no remote features and harder temporary access management.

Best Practical Approach

Many homes benefit from hybrid security: a quality deadbolt plus a professionally installed smart lock where convenience is needed.
