Happy Christmas from CCRL Locksmith! 🎄

By CCR Locksmith Team • Published December 24, 2025 • Updated March 4, 2026

From all of us at CCR Locksmith, happy holidays and thank you for trusting us throughout the year. Christmas week usually brings extra travel, package deliveries, and late-night returns home, which can increase lockout and security issues.

Holiday Security Checklist

  • Test your exterior locks before travel, especially side and garage entries.
  • Use timers for interior lights so the home does not appear vacant.
  • Avoid hiding spare keys in obvious outdoor spots.
  • Confirm your car key battery and keep a backup key in a safe location.
  • Ask a trusted neighbor to collect visible packages.

If You Get Locked Out

Stay in a safe, visible area and call a licensed locksmith. For your protection, we verify authorization before service and provide upfront pricing before work begins.
