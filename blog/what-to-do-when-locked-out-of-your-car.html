What to Do When Locked Out of Your Car

By CCR Locksmith Team • Published December 18, 2025 • Updated March 4, 2026

Step 1: Stay Safe

If you are in traffic, bad weather, or a dark area, move to a safer spot and keep your phone charged.

Step 2: Check All Doors

Try each door and trunk. Sometimes one entry point remains unlocked.

Step 3: Avoid DIY Damage

Coat hangers and forced-entry attempts often damage weather stripping, paint, and electronic systems.

Step 4: Call a Licensed Locksmith

Provide vehicle make/model/year and your exact location. Ask for upfront pricing before dispatch.
