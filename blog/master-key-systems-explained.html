Master Key Systems Explained: Benefits for Your Business

By CCR Locksmith Team • Published December 15, 2025 • Updated March 4, 2026

A master key system creates access layers so owners/managers can open multiple doors while staff access is limited to assigned areas.

Why Businesses Use It

  • Fewer keys to manage.
  • Clearer access control by role.
  • Faster response to staffing changes.
  • Better documentation and key accountability.

Best Practices

  • Keep written authorization list for key duplication.
  • Use restricted keyways when appropriate.
  • Review access hierarchy annually.
