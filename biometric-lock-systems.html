High-Security Biometric Solutions

Biometric locks offer the ultimate in security and convenience by using unique biological traits for identification. CCR Locksmith provides cutting-edge biometric lock installation for businesses and high-security residential properties. Forget about lost keys or forgotten codes—your fingerprint or face is the key.

We install a range of biometric devices, from standalone fingerprint deadbolts to networked systems integrated with broader access control. Our experts will help you choose the right technology for your specific security needs and environment.

Types of Biometric Locks We Install:

  • Fingerprint Scanners: Quick and reliable access using your unique fingerprint.
  • Facial Recognition: Touchless entry systems that recognize authorized personnel.
  • Retina/Iris Scanners: Ultra-high security for sensitive areas.
  • Hybrid Systems: Combine biometrics with keypads or cards for multi-factor authentication.

Benefits of Biometrics

  • Uncopyable Keys: Biological traits cannot be lost, stolen, or duplicated like keys.
  • Audit Capabilities: Track exactly who accessed a door and when.
  • Convenience: No need to carry keys or remember complex passwords.
  • Modern Appeal: Enhance the technological profile of your facility.
