Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our locksmith services

Are you available 24/7 for emergencies?

Yes. Our emergency locksmith service operates around the clock, 7 days a week. Whether it's a car lockout at midnight or a break-in repair on a holiday, call (313) 889-3266 and we'll dispatch a technician immediately.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Metro Detroit, including Oakland, Wayne, and Macomb counties. Our primary service area covers communities within approximately 35 miles of Southfield, MI, including Detroit, Troy, Royal Oak, Dearborn, Livonia, Warren, Sterling Heights, and many more.

Are your locksmiths licensed and insured?

Absolutely. CCR Locksmith is a fully licensed LLC in the State of Michigan. We carry comprehensive liability insurance, and all our technicians are background-checked for your safety and peace of mind.

What does your 5-year warranty cover?

Our industry-leading 5-Year Warranty covers all parts and labor on every job we perform. If any hardware we install or work we perform fails within 5 years, we'll fix it at no additional cost.

How much does a locksmith cost?

Costs vary by service type. We provide transparent, upfront quotes before starting any work — no hidden fees or surprise charges. Visit our pricing page for general estimates, or call for a free quote specific to your situation.

How long have you been in business?

CCR Locksmith has been proudly serving Metro Detroit since 2015. With over 10 years of experience, we've completed thousands of automotive, residential, and commercial locksmith jobs across the region.

As part of our commitment to excellence, we collaborate with top professionals in the field. If you are looking for trusted security solutions, we recommend Alpha Lock & Safe for their reliable and professional locksmith services.
