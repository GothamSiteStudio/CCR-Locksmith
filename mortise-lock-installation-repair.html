Mortise Lock Installation & Repair

Specialized service for heavy-duty and vintage mortise locksets.

Mortise locks are known for their durability and security, often found in commercial buildings and older residential homes. Unlike standard cylindrical locks, mortise locks are recessed into the door edge, requiring specialized knowledge to install and repair. CCR Locksmith has the expertise to handle these complex locking mechanisms.

Our Mortise Lock Services

  • Installation: Precise cutting and fitting for new mortise lock installations on wood or metal doors.
  • Repair: Fixing internal components like springs, levers, and bolts to restore smooth operation.
  • Cylinder Replacement: Upgrading the key cylinder to high-security or restricted keyway options.
  • Hardware Upgrades: Replacing worn-out handles, knobs, and trim with modern or period-correct hardware.

Why Choose Us for Mortise Locks?

Mortise locks are intricate and expensive. Improper handling can damage the door or the lock itself. Our technicians are trained in the mechanics of major brands like Sargent, Corbin Russwin, Yale, and Baldwin, ensuring your lock is serviced correctly the first time.
