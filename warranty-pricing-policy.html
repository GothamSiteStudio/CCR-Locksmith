Warranty & Pricing Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

Transparent Pricing

  • We provide an upfront estimate before work begins.
  • Final price may change if on-site conditions differ materially from the original description.
  • No work is performed beyond approved scope without customer authorization.

Service Call and Emergency Rates

Pricing may vary by service type, hardware requirements, complexity, and travel distance. Emergency or after-hours service may include additional charges when disclosed in advance.

5-Year Warranty Coverage

Our stated 5-year warranty applies to eligible parts and labor installed/performed by CCR Locksmith, subject to proper use and normal conditions.

  • Covered: workmanship defects and eligible installed hardware failures during warranty period.
  • Not covered: misuse, vandalism, break-ins, acts of nature, unauthorized third-party modifications, normal wear items, or pre-existing non-serviced components.

Warranty Claims

To request warranty service, call (313) 889-3266 with service address and approximate original service date. We will verify eligibility and schedule inspection/remedy.

Cancellations and Rescheduling

If you need to cancel or reschedule, contact us as early as possible. A dispatch/diagnostic fee may apply when a technician has already been assigned or arrived.

Refunds

If you believe there is a billing error or unresolved service issue, contact us promptly. Approved refunds or adjustments are handled after review of the job details and completed work scope.
