Door Closer Installation & Repair

Keep your commercial doors operating smoothly and safely.

A functioning door closer is essential for the security, safety, and energy efficiency of your business. It ensures that fire doors latch correctly, prevents doors from slamming, and helps maintain climate control. CCR Locksmith provides expert installation, adjustment, and repair services for all types of commercial door closers.

Common Door Closer Issues We Fix

  • Slamming Doors: Adjusting hydraulic pressure to prevent injury and damage.
  • Leaking Fluid: Replacing seals or the entire unit if the hydraulic fluid is leaking.
  • Door Not Latching: Fine-tuning the closing speed and latching action for security.
  • Broken Arms: Replacing bent or snapped closer arms.

Types of Closers We Service

We work with surface-mounted, concealed, and floor-spring door closers from top manufacturers like LCN, Norton, and Dorma. Whether you need a heavy-duty closer for a high-traffic entrance or an ADA-compliant automatic operator, we have the solution.
