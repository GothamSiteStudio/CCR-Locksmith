About CCR Locksmith: Your Trusted Metro Detroit Locksmith Since 2015

Welcome to CCR Locksmith, your reliable partner for all your locksmith needs in Southfield and the greater Metro Detroit area. Since 2015, Car Commercial Residential Emergency Locksmith Service, LLC has been committed to providing fast, professional, and dependable locksmith services.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to deliver top-quality locksmith solutions with honesty and integrity. We understand that when you're facing a lockout or need to enhance your security, you need a service you can trust. That's why we back every job with our industry-leading 5-Year Warranty on all parts and labor.

Over a Decade of Hands-On Locksmith Experience

With over 10 years of hands-on experience in the locksmith industry, our team has successfully completed thousands of service calls across Metro Detroit. From late-night car lockouts on I-696 to complete commercial access control installations for office buildings, we've seen — and solved — it all.

Our real-world experience means we arrive prepared for any situation. We've worked with every major vehicle brand, including Ford, GM, Chrysler, Toyota, Honda, and luxury brands like BMW and Mercedes-Benz. For residential clients, we've secured single-family homes, condos, and apartment complexes with everything from basic rekeying to advanced smart lock systems.

Professional Expertise & Training

Locksmithing is a skilled trade that requires continuous learning. Our technicians stay current with the latest security technologies, including:

  • Transponder & Smart Key Programming: Factory-level programming for modern vehicle keys and fobs.
  • High-Security Lock Systems: Installation and servicing of Medeco, Mul-T-Lock, Abloy, and other restricted keyway systems.
  • Electronic Access Control: Design and installation of card reader, keypad, and biometric access systems for commercial properties.
  • Safe & Vault Services: Professional safe opening, combination changing, and installation.
  • Smart Home Security: Integration of smart locks, Wi-Fi-enabled deadbolts, and home automation security systems.

We invest in professional-grade tools and diagnostic equipment to ensure every job is done right the first time.
