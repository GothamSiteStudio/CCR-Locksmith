Electronic Keypad Lock Installation

Convenience and security without the keys.

Stop worrying about lost keys or lockouts. Electronic keypad locks offer a modern, secure, and convenient way to access your home or business. CCR Locksmith specializes in the installation and programming of high-quality electronic locks from top brands.

Benefits of Keypad Locks

  • Keyless Convenience: Enter your home with a simple code. No more fumbling for keys.
  • Multiple User Codes: Create unique codes for family members, guests, or employees.
  • Easy Access Control: Add or delete codes instantly without changing the lock.
  • Auto-Lock Features: Set your door to lock automatically after a few seconds for peace of mind.

Our Installation Process

We don't just install the hardware; we ensure you know how to use it. Our service includes:

  1. Consultation: Helping you choose the right lock (deadbolt vs. lever, touchscreen vs. buttons).
  2. Professional Installation: Ensuring the lock is fitted correctly and aligns perfectly with the strike plate.
  3. Programming Setup: We help you set your master code and user codes.
  4. Demonstration: We show you how to change batteries and manage codes.
